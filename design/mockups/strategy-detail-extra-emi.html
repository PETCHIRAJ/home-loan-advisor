Step-by-Step Implementation
  1. Calculate Your Extra EMI Amount
    Divide your current EMI by 12. For ₹25,436 EMI, extra payment = ₹2,120 per month
  2. Set Up Automatic Transfer
    Create a separate savings account and auto-transfer ₹2,120 monthly
  3. Make Annual Prepayment
    Every 12 months, use accumulated ₹25,440 for loan prepayment
  4. Choose Tenure Reduction
    Opt for tenure reduction instead of EMI reduction for maximum savings
  5. Track Your Progress
    Monitor savings using our progress tracker and celebrate milestones
🎯
Pro Tips for Success
  • Start the strategy right after your next EMI due date for clean accounting
  • Use bonus, tax refund, or increment money for the first extra payment to kickstart
  • Set phone reminders for annual prepayment - don't let the money sit idle
  • Inform your bank in advance about annual prepayment to avoid processing delays
  • Keep track of prepayment certificates for tax benefits under Section 24(b)
⚠️
Important Considerations
  • Ensure you have 6-month emergency fund before starting this strategy
  • Check if your bank charges prepayment fees (most don't for self-occupied property)
  • This strategy works best if you plan to hold the loan for more than 5 years
  • Consider your other investment opportunities - if you can earn >8.5% elsewhere, invest instead
  • Some months might be tight - maintain flexibility to skip if needed
